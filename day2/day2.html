Day 2: Into the Atlas

Back to Home

Day two took us out of the city and into the raw beauty of the Atlas Mountains. A hired jeep carved its way through winding roads, unveiling jagged cliffs and terraced villages perched like brushstrokes across the hills.

We visited Aït Benhaddou, a UNESCO-protected ksar where history and film mythos converge. The air felt older here—dry, sacred. Even the stones whispered.
