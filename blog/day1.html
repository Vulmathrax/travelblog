Day 1: Touchdown in Marrakech

Back to Home

Our journey begins in the vivid heart of Morocco. Stepping off the plane in Marrakech, we were immediately immersed in a tapestry of scents, colors, and sounds. The city pulses with life—from the rhythmic claps of Gnawa music to the golden haze of spice stalls in Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

After checking into a local riad, we wandered through narrow alleyways, losing ourselves in the Medina’s mosaic beauty. Friendly locals guided us to a rooftop café where we sipped mint tea and watched the sun melt behind minarets.
